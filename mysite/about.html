About Clean Sweep

Redefining cleanliness standards since 2009

Our Story

Clean Sweep Cleaning Services was founded in 2009 in Champaign, Illinois, with a simple mission: to redefine cleanliness standards for homes and businesses throughout Champaign County.

For over 15 years, we have been dedicated to the belief that cleaning is a craft. This means paying meticulous attention to every corner, crevice, and surface - creating environments that are not just visually appealing, but deeply sanitized and healthy.

What started as a small local cleaning service has grown into a trusted name in residential and commercial cleaning across Champaign, Urbana, Savoy, Mahomet, and Rantoul.
